Melanin — Nature's Golden Polymer

A unique biopolymer with growing demand in modern industries

What is Melanin?

Melanin is a natural pigment formed from tyrosine. It protects against UV radiation, acts as an antioxidant, and binds heavy metals. The three main types are: eumelanin, pheomelanin, and neuromelanin.

Sources

Melanin can be extracted from:

  • 🪲 Hermetia illucens larvae (black soldier fly)
  • 🍄 Fungi (e.g., Aspergillus niger)
  • 🧬 Human skin, hair, and eyes
  • 🧫 Bacteria and algae

Applications

Used in:

  • 💄 Cosmetics — as UV filters and dyes
  • 💊 Pharmaceuticals — antioxidants and radioprotectors
  • 🔬 Biotechnology — implants and biosensors
  • 🔌 Electronics — organic conductive components
