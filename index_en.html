What is Melanin?

Melanin is a natural pigment synthesized from the amino acid tyrosine. It plays a crucial role in UV protection, has antioxidant properties, and can even conduct electricity. The three main types are eumelanin, pheomelanin, and neuromelanin. Melanin is increasingly seen as a universal functional material of the future.
